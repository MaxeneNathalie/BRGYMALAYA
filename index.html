ABOUT US

Meet Our Dedicated Team

Our barangay is fortunate to have a team of dedicated and committed individuals serving as our councilors and chairman. Their leadership and guidance have been instrumental in the progress and development of our community. We extend our sincere gratitude to them for their unwavering commitment to public service. They are committed to serve the needs of our community. Offering a wide range of essential services, including administrative assistance, social services, infrastructure maintenance, community outreach, and disaster preparedness. Our team is always available to assist you with any inquiries or concerns you may have.

OUR SERVICES

Important Documents

Assistance in processing essential documents like birth certificates, barangay clearances, and other certifications needed for legal and personal matters.

Free Medicine and Vitamins

We offer free distribution of basic medicines and vitamins to ensure the health and well-being of our residents, especially for the elderly, pregnant women, and children.

Free Printing

Residents can avail of free printing services for necessary documents, easing the burden for those who require official paperwork for school, work, or government transactions.

Community Outreach Programs

Regular programs aimed at enhancing community engagement and providing necessary social services to those in need.
